About the Artist

Answering your W's!

Who is Shangu?

She is a self-taught artist from the Philippines. 'Shangu' is a pen-name inspired by her real name 'Shannel'. She started creating and sharing her artworks ever since the pandemic started, though she has been learning her inks and paints since high school.

Why does she draw?

Lines and colors were always a fascination for her ever since she started watching and seeing animations. The moment she found out that she, too, can have the possibilty to create wondrous and captivating drawings, she didn't hesitate to learn it.

What can she do?

With her passion to learn from zero, Shangu can now show her skills through traditional watercolor artworks, traditional gouache paintings, digital illustrations on stylized portraits and a little 'bit of background drawings.
